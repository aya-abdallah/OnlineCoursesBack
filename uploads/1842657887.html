Editorial Reviews

From the Back Cover

FUNDAMENTALS OF INFORMATION TECHNOLOGYcomprehensively covers both the basic and advanced aspects of InformationTechnology. The book starts with a simple but comprehensive discussion of basicconcepts of Information Technology as well as Computer Science. It explains thevarious common input/output devices along with a few advanced and rarely useones in detail. The book also discusses a majority of the widely used applicationsoftwares with its area of application and includes review questions toreinforce and enhance learning. Basics to advanced topics of computer networksare presented in the computer networks section.
The book is written in areader-friendly style the book is primarily designed to meet the needs of theunder-graduate students of several disciplines.

Salient Features
Detailed elucidation on various semiconductor memories.
Dedicated chapter for the Internet.
From basics to complex levels of Binary Arithmetic.

About the Author

Anoop Mathew is an writer, engineer and professor of a reputed self-financing engineering college in Kerala, India. He was born in a Syrian Christian family in Calicut, Kerala state, India. He was graduated in Electronics Engineering and was awarded his doctoral degree in Electrical Engineering.
He is the Recipient of "Best Engineering Faculty Award-2012" and Best Application Paper Award in PowerCon-2010 for the paper - SMPS based on Matrix Converter with reduced number of Switches.
He is a member of Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers (IEEE), Indian Society for Technical Education (ISTE), Energy Conservation Society (ECS), Computer Society of India (CSI), Association for Computing Machinery (ACM), International Association of Computer Science and Information Technology (IACSIT), International Association of Engineers (IAENG) and life member of Conservation Society (ECS) and International Association of Computer Science and Information Technology (IACSIT). He organized a few national level workshops and seminars.
Anoop Mathew is an editorial board member of "International Journal of Computer and Electrical Technology" and has published more than 10 research papers in leading international journals and 3 papers in Technical Conferences. He has delivered a few invited talks on Matrix Converters and Switched Mode Power Converters in various engineering colleges in India.

S. Kavitha Murugeshan.: Department of Computer Science and Engineering Vedvyasa Institute of Technology, Malappuram
